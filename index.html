About Us

Sunwater is a pioneering company founded with a mission to blend innovation and environmental responsibility in the creation of high-quality, eco-friendly water bottles. Our journey began several years ago when a group of dedicated environmentalists and entrepreneurs came together with a shared vision: to combat plastic pollution and promote sustainable living through practical, everyday solutions.

The inception of Sunwater was driven by the alarming rise in plastic waste, particularly in our oceans. Observing the increasing amounts of plastic debris choking marine ecosystems and harming wildlife, we were inspired to take action. We realized that addressing this issue required not only raising awareness but also developing tangible solutions. Thus, Sunwater was born with the goal of transforming recycled ocean plastic into durable, functional products.

Our unique approach involves collecting plastic waste from the Pacific Ocean, which is then processed and repurposed into high-quality water bottles. This innovative method serves a dual purpose: it helps reduce the volume of plastic pollution in our seas and provides consumers with sustainable alternatives to single-use plastics. By engaging in this process, we actively contribute to cleaning up our oceans and minimizing the environmental footprint of plastic production.

Choosing Sunwater means supporting a company that stands at the forefront of the fight against plastic pollution. Our water bottles are designed with a focus on both environmental impact and user convenience. They are reusable and recyclable, offering an effective solution to reduce reliance on disposable plastics. Depending on the model, our bottles can accommodate a range of beverages, from hot drinks like coffee and tea to cold refreshments such as water and juices. This versatility ensures that you can enjoy your favorite drinks at the perfect temperature, whether you're at home, at work, or on the go.
