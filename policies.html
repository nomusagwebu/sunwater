Policies

Financing:

1. Equity Financing
Angel Investors: We seek investments from angel investors who are passionate about sustainability and eco-friendly products. In exchange for their investment, they receive ownership equity or convertible debt.
Venture Capital: We partner with venture capital firms that specialize in eco-friendly ventures, ensuring that we not only receive substantial funding but also valuable strategic guidance.

2. Debt Financing
Bank Loans: We obtain traditional bank loans with competitive interest rates, leveraging company assets as collateral to secure the necessary capital for growth.
Microloans: We utilize microloan programs tailored for small businesses, offered by nonprofit organizations or government agencies, to support our financing needs.

3. Crowdfunding
Reward-Based Crowdfunding: We engage with platforms like Kickstarter or Indiegogo to pre-sell our eco-friendly water bottles and raise funds from early supporters in exchange for exclusive products and rewards. Equity Crowdfunding: We offer small equity stakes in our company through equity crowdfunding platforms, allowing a broader audience to invest in our mission and products.

4. Government Grants and Subsidies
Environmental Grants: We apply for grants and subsidies from government agencies and environmental organizations dedicated to supporting sustainable and eco-friendly initiatives.

5. Trade Credit
Supplier Financing: We negotiate favorable payment terms with our suppliers, enabling us to defer payments for raw materials or inventory purchases, which helps in managing cash flow.

6. Leasing
Equipment Leasing: We lease manufacturing equipment rather than purchasing it outright, which conserves cash flow while ensuring we have the necessary production capabilities.

7. Retained Earnings
Reinvestment: We reinvest our profits back into the company, funding growth initiatives and minimizing the need for external financing while focusing on sustainable expansion.

8. Invoice Financing
Factoring: We sell our accounts receivable to factoring companies at a discount to quickly obtain cash, improving liquidity and operational flexibility.

9. Green Bonds
Issuing Green Bonds: We raise capital by issuing green bonds specifically designed to fund environmentally friendly projects, appealing to socially responsible investors.

10. Strategic Partnerships
Joint Ventures: We form strategic partnerships or joint ventures with like-minded companies to share resources and co-finance projects, aligning with our commitment to sustainability.

Delivery:

1. Standard Shipping
Reliable and Affordable: Our standard shipping option ensures that your order arrives within 5-7 business days. We use reliable carriers to deliver your eco-friendly water bottles safely and affordably.

2. Expedited Shipping
Fast and Efficient: Need your water bottles sooner? Choose our expedited shipping option for delivery within 2-3 business days. We prioritize speed and efficiency without compromising on quality.

3. International Shipping
Global Reach: We offer international shipping to bring our eco-friendly water bottles to customers worldwide. Delivery times and rates vary by destination, and we provide tracking information for all international orders.

4. Eco-Friendly Packaging
Sustainable and Minimal: We are committed to sustainability in every aspect of our business. Our water bottles are shipped in eco-friendly packaging made from recycled materials, reducing waste and our environmental footprint.

5. Order Tracking
Stay Informed: Once your order is shipped, you'll receive a tracking number via email. Use this number to track your package in real-time and stay updated on its delivery status from our warehouse to your doorstep.

Return Policies:

1. 30-Day Satisfaction Guarantee
Hassle-Free Returns: We stand by the quality of our eco-friendly water bottles. If you are not completely satisfied with your purchase, you can return the product within 30 days of receiving it for a full refund or exchange. Simply contact our customer service team to initiate the return process, and we’ll provide you with a prepaid return label.

2. Defective or Damaged Items
Quality Assurance: If you receive a defective or damaged product, please let us know within 14 days of delivery. We will promptly replace the item at no additional cost to you. Contact our customer service team with your order number and a photo of the damaged or defective item, and we’ll take care of the rest.
